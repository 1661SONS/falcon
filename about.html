EMPATHY.

I define empathy as the fragile relationship between gatekeeping trust and optimizing deep appreciation. It is compassionate evangelism on the behalf of others. It is hustling to remain intuitively connected to real people – and making sure they it.

ARCHITECTURE.

Where beauty meets purpose; I believe this is where UX and Engineering become one and the same. Successful product designers master this principle, while maintaining an unmistakable identity. The iterations that emerge from IA and IX Design demonstrate whether or not you were listening.

PROTOTYPING.

The Scientific Method comes in handy here. I remind myself to make very few (if any) assumptions, to ask a lot of questions, and make sure that data backs me up, not my imagination. This is sink or swim, but where floaties are better than scuba gear. Failure is necessary to move forward – so pack light.

CAMARADERIE.

One of my favorite aspects of being a designer is the community's encouragement of information sharing. This comes with the territory. to find success in design, you have to be open and honest. From Developers to Stakeholders, we UXers take everything into account while attempting to preach digital prophecy – and I it.
