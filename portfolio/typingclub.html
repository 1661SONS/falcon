TYPING CLUB.

strict information architecture for technical education - Typing Club is the best online tool available for teaching kids how to touch type

"actually, we have a wireframe for that too.."
  • Client: Typing Club. / Date: Summer 2014. / Category: Information Architecture, Wireframes for Days.

Typing Club is the most intuitive web application for learning how to type.. The Competitive Analysis tells no lies. However, the interface was not so intuitive for the teachers. The project was a major excavation in that we had to dig deep to the bottom, find what teachers what looking for, and bring it up to the surface. The task was initially more daunting than exciting. The client requested a complete overhaul of the teacher, student, and administrative portals. However, the hammer had to be brought down in recognition of the 3-week time crunch.

We used several Card Sorting sessions to narrow down the ask, and focus our attention on the chief users â€“ teachers. We were able to conduct an impressive amount of user interviews, and received survey answers from Seattle to New Zealand. The experience of these teachers ranged from one year to twenty-five years, while their responses displayed an array of school types (public, private, charter, etc.). Though there was a clear pattern for wanting an easier way to manage their lesson plans, the teachers' responses were quite divided on how they interacted with students, parents, and administrative staff. Because of this, we created personas of varying teaching styles, responsibilities, and resources to best reflect our dynamic. This set the tone for our design studio with the client, where every idea was backed by teacher input.
