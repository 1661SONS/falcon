Never underestimate the power of a strong client relationship. The challenges associated with this project were tied to technical constraints more than anything else (see 'fauxdal' above). However, these constraints were tackled without hesitation, and as a full team. From PMs to Drupal experts, many brains came together for impromptu design studios and on-the-spot collaboration.

Creating internal prototypes (built with Axure) for an external and out-of-state devteam to build presented a unique opportunity to foster an environment built on honest communication and trust. Requirements updates were spread almost immediately, leaving little room for confusion and design debt.
