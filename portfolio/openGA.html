openGA - Katalyst - Just Startup.

from openGA to Katalyst to Just Startup: a pre-accelerator focused on the Intrapreneurs of DC's tech community

Just Startup was founded by a passionate group of General Assembly alumni on a mission to transform notions into thriving ventures. We saw ideas go idle for lack of structure and community. We set out to fill that gap with mentorship, promise and compassion.

  • Project: openGA / General Assembly DC Hackathon turned Startup. / Date: November 2014 - April 2015. / Category: UX Lead, Founding Member

You'd think that solving a problem for a user group that you're apart of would be quite easy, right? Nah.. Here, the problem was known too well. Although we were the users, we had to constantly resist designing for ourselves while assuming those solutions would work for everyone. Luckily, the Washington, DC General Assembly campus is a goldmine of students and alumni in our shoes, but everyone ties them differently. What we had was an overflow of people who shared our problem, but not how they would want it solved.

Most accelerators focus on what your team can do developmentally - with the right tools. We focused on what you could do with people - with the right people. Our biggest challenge was fear: 'What if they're not ready?' .. 'What if we're not ready?' Convinving others was not the blocker. Creating a stellar in-person component that folks would actually be apart of was. We were creating a educational hackathon for the futures of others' products, not just a weekend hobby.
